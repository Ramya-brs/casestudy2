Online Food Delivery App Info(Zomato vs Swiggy)

Objective


As we all Know that Online Food Ordering is the process of ordering food, delivery or pickup, from a website or other applications.

OBJECTIVE OF THE PROJECT INCLUDES

1. To analyze and understand which online food delivery app is being used among Swiggy and Zomato.

2. To analyze the age groups of the people using which app to order their food.

3. To know various types of food available in two different apps(Swiggy and Zomato).

4. To analyze which app gives the on-time delivery.

5. To analyze the most preferred food delivery app by the gender

6. To identify how much expenditure spent by people on ordering their food in(Zomato&Swiggy).

Technology Stack


Python



Python is a high-level programming language valued for its readability and versatility, widely used in web development, data analysis, machine learning, and automation, with a large library of pre-built functions and packages.


R Language



R is a programming language and environment for statistical computing and data analysis, featuring a rich ecosystem of packages and visualizations widely used by statisticians and data scientists. It excels in data manipulation, analysis, and visualization.

Challenges

I gathered the responses from 500 people by creating a google form based on what is the age, what is the app they prefer to order the food from, what type of food they like to eat etc. And after I applied the graphical and statistical analysis like correlation and chi-square on each term, here I face a challenge to analyse those terms because of having an Unstructured Format.

Solutions


For getting accurate information we need to know sampling strategies, and ethical considerations to ensure the reliability and validity of the data collected.


And need to provide "prefer not to say" or "skip" options for sensitive questions like income, location etc in Forms.


We all need to collect a sample data before doing statistical project for better accurate results.

Result


Finally, Resulted that the information gathered and operations done on Statistical and Graphical analysis is-.


"Zomato is the most preferred Online Food Delivery App Compared to Swiggy".
